# Maintenance & Evolution Pack Index

## Skills
- **corrective-adaptive-perfective-preventive-classifier** — Classify maintenance work as corrective, adaptive, perfective, or preventive so the team can choose the right response and priority.
- **bug-reproduction-writer** — Write clear, deterministic bug reproduction steps with environment, data, and expected versus actual behavior.
- **maintenance-triage-helper** — Triage maintenance work by severity, urgency, user impact, risk, and likely ownership.
- **root-cause-hypothesis-generator** — Generate plausible root-cause hypotheses from symptoms, recent changes, and system context without overstating certainty.
- **change-impact-mapper** — Map likely downstream impacts of a proposed change across code, data, interfaces, users, and operations.
- **regression-risk-reviewer** — Review a maintenance change for regression risk and identify the most exposed behaviors, integrations, and environments.
- **hotfix-vs-patch-vs-version-update-selector** — Select whether an issue should be handled as a hotfix, normal patch, or broader version update based on urgency and blast radius.
- **rollback-plan-writer** — Write a rollback plan that defines reversal triggers, prerequisites, data concerns, and communication steps.
- **deprecation-planner** — Plan a controlled deprecation path for features, APIs, components, or configurations without surprising consumers.
- **migration-readiness-checker** — Check whether a system, component, or dataset is ready for migration with acceptable technical and operational risk.
- **maintenance-knowledge-capture-writer** — Capture maintenance knowledge into reusable notes, decision records, runbooks, and future guardrails.
